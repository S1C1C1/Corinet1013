About Us

Our Mission:

At Corinet1013, our mission is to empower Christian parents and pastors with the tools and knowledge necessary to protect their families and congregations from the myriad of online temptations. We strive to blend faith-based principles with practical advice to foster environments where individuals can thrive spiritually and digitally.

Our Vision:

We envision a world where Christian families and church communities navigate the internet confidently and safely, free from harmful influences, and grounded in their faith.
